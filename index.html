Our Goals

Mission 1

We want to involve the fans from day one. So they have the opportunity to name their team to pick their colors and that’s what’s important to us and that’s why we haven’t announced everything today.

Mission 2

We know the right people are in place and the time is right for Miami to become a great Major League Soccer city. The fans have driven us this far, and the believe in the club we are trying to create. Our main goal is to win, and to win right out of the gates.

Accomplishments as a Player
He is the first English player to win league titles in four countries: England, Spain, the United States and France. He announced his retirement in May 2013 after a 20-year career, during which he won 19 major trophies.
Impact on the Game
Known for his range of passing, crossing ability and bending free-kicks as a right winger, Beckham was runner up in the Ballon d'Or, twice runner-up for FIFA World Player of the Year and in 2004 he was named by Pelé in the FIFA 100 list of the world's greatest living players. He was inducted into the English Football Hall of Fame in 2008. A global ambassador for the sport, Beckham is regarded as a British cultural icon.
Major League Soccer Team in Miami
On 5 February 2014, MLS announced that Beckham had exercised his option to buy a MLS expansion team for $25 million, which he had received as part of the contract he signed with the LA Galaxy in 2007. The ownership group, led by Beckham, originally hoped the Miami-based team would begin play in 2016 or 2017. After delays getting a stadium deal completed, MLS announced in January 2018 that the team had been approved and would likely begin play in 2020.
